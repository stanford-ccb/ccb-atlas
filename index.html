About this site

This web atlas is designed to provide a guide to the rich biological diversity in Mesoamerica, one of the worlds key biodiversity hotspots. It is maintained by the Center for Conservation Biology at Stanford University, and primarily developed by Maxwell Patterson. You can reach Maxwell via email here. We hope you enjoy browsing!
